For the world premiere of NEON's The Monkey, Flux transformed a 1920s Gothic Revival church — with its 205-ft bell tower glowing blood red — into the ultimate horror cinema experience. Guests paid their respects to Uncle Chip before entering the sanctuary, where Blasting Company led a haunting procession of Chopin's funeral march past packed pews. Responsibilities

  • Worked on event welcome logistics, performers, popcorn, and candy giveaways
  • Coordinated on-site event tasks and logistics with team leads
  • Worked on installation and strike of event production and decor
